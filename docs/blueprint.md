# **App Name**: TechVault Mobiles

## Core Features:

- User Authentication & Profiles: Secure user registration and login via email/password and Google. Users can manage their personal information, delivery addresses, and view their order history.
- Product Catalog & Search: Browse a comprehensive catalog of mobile phones with filtering options by brand, price, specifications (RAM, storage), and more. Includes a robust search bar for instant results.
- Product Details & Reviews: Detailed product pages with multiple images, full specifications, pricing (including discounts), and user reviews. Users can submit reviews with ratings and comments.
- Shopping Cart Management: Users can add/remove products from their cart, adjust quantities, and see a running total. The cart state is persisted across sessions.
- Order Placement & History: Streamlined checkout process allowing users to confirm shipping details and place orders. Users can view past orders with their current status.
- AI Phone Recommender Tool: An AI-powered tool that assists users in finding the perfect mobile phone based on their budget, desired features, and usage patterns by asking clarifying questions.
- Promotions and Banners Display: Display active promotional banners and featured products dynamically on the homepage to highlight deals and new arrivals.

## Style Guidelines:

- The visual scheme embraces a dark mode aesthetic, conveying a sophisticated and high-tech feel suitable for a mobile store. The primary interactive color is a dynamic, deep violet-blue, `#6F2AED`, chosen for its modern appeal and energetic contrast against dark backgrounds. The background color is a subtle, deep charcoal with a hint of purple, `#271F2E`, ensuring a premium visual experience that reduces eye strain. The accent color, a bright and clean sky blue, `#B0D6FF`, provides clear emphasis for calls to action and important alerts, offering a fresh, analogous contrast.
- Headlines will utilize 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), lending a crisp, futuristic, and tech-forward character to prominent text. Body text will use 'Inter' (sans-serif), a highly legible and versatile font chosen for its modern, neutral aesthetic, ensuring a comfortable reading experience across product descriptions and reviews.
- Use clean, minimalist line icons throughout the interface to maintain a sleek, modern, and uncluttered appearance. Icons should be easily understandable and align with the technological theme of the application.
- Employ a responsive, modular grid layout system to ensure products and content are presented elegantly and are accessible across various screen sizes. Focus on ample negative space to highlight product imagery and essential information.
- Implement subtle and smooth transition animations for navigation, product card hovers, and additions to the shopping cart. These micro-interactions should feel fluid and contribute to a premium user experience without being distracting.
